Book List
Lava Masters

This series has three books. It is a science fiction story.

Book One

Criminals all over the world are on the loose, and police need help to track them down. Kenny and his band of lava masters are hired by the police to round up all the prisoners. As the lava masters design special transportation and inescapable prison cells at the lava palace the criminals begin to fear being captured and spread out to different countries. Kenny and his lava master crew must track them down quickly to restore order.

Book Two

Kenny and his band of lava masters are at it again only this time the start of their adventure is thwarted by a surprise disappearance. The team of lava masters must solve a series of mysteries to fully understand all that is involved in preventing the expedition. Will they get to report back to NASA?

Book Three

In this third instalment of the Lava Masters series, the team of lava masters tackle a series of adventures ending in a battle against the greatest criminal organization in the world. Who will succeed?
